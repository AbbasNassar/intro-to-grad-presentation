Why?

Remote Vehicle Start and Climate Control

Abbas can start his vehicle remotely using the mobile app, allowing the car’s air conditioning or heating system to create a comfortable interior before he gets in. This feature is especially helpful for Abbas during extreme weather, ensuring comfort and saving him time.

Real-Time Security Alerts and Remote Locking

Ghassan receives instant notifications on his phone if suspicious activity is detected, such as unauthorized door access or movement. He can immediately respond by remotely locking the doors or disabling the ignition, protecting his vehicle from theft or unauthorized use.

Live Vehicle Health Monitoring

Mohammad is able to monitor real time data about his car’s status including fuel level, battery health, tire pressure, and maintenance alerts directly through the mobile application. This allows Mohammad to proactively address issues, reduce the risk of breakdowns, and schedule maintenance efficiently.

Future Work

Voice Recognition

Integrate voice recognition technology to enable hands-free control of vehicle systems. This will allow users to operate functions such as media, navigation, and climate control using spoken commands, improving safety and user experience by reducing manual interactions.

Proof of Concept Implementation

In the next phase, we will apply our system on a real vehicle by enabling remote engine start, which helps users protect their cars engine even when traveling abroad.

Advanced Safety Features

We will integrate an oxygen sensor to monitor the air quality inside the car. This feature is designed to protect children who may be accidentally left inside the vehicle; if the oxygen level drops below a safe threshold, the system will automatically alert the relevant authorities, potentially saving lives.
